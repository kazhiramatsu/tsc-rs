# M1 re-review (2026-07-22) — verified findings and the pre-M7 debt ledger

An external re-review of the M1 scanner/parser was verified claim-by-claim
on 2026-07-22 (all mechanical claims confirmed; two impact claims
corrected, see below). Two slices landed in response:

- **prefix-determinism UTF-16 fix** (PR #59): the invariant compared a
  UTF-8 byte cut against UTF-16 token ends — harness false positive on
  non-ASCII fixtures, reproduced at `--limit 1000` before the fix.
- **schema restore** (this slice): the d.ts extractor's broken
  qualifier-stripping meant the extraction was effectively dead — struct
  payloads came from hand seeds, three interfaces leaked 19 bogus
  `readonly_*` fields, and five tsc fields had no slot
  (`HeritageClause.token`, `ImportType.isTypeOf`,
  `MetaProperty.keywordToken`, `JsxText.containsOnlyTriviaWhiteSpaces`,
  `ImportClause.phaseModifier`). The extractor now parses the d.ts for
  real (admission-listed payloads, alias resolution, claimant
  tie-breaks), the checker's three source-text re-derivations are
  retired, and the **schema-audit gate** (`cargo xtask schema-audit`,
  `crates/oracle/schema-dump.mjs` — the vendored TypeScript parsing its
  own d.ts) cross-checks every generated field; remaining field debt is
  machine-tracked in `tsrs2/nodes-missing-fields.txt`.

  A second review round caught two restore gaps, fixed as follow-ups on
  the same slice: (1) child-field optionality was fabricated (`optional:
  true` across the board — 232 d.ts-required children, `BinaryExpression.
  left` included, wrongly marked optional, and schema-audit ignored the
  bit). Children now merge from the d.ts with their real optionality and
  schema-audit compares it (232 → 0). The RUST layout deliberately keeps
  every child Option-typed (debt row 8 below). (2) the four
  template-literal `rawText` payloads (`NoSubstitutionTemplateLiteral`,
  `TemplateHead/Middle/Tail`) were admitted into the schema but the
  parser pinned them `None`; they now capture tsc's
  `getTemplateLiteralRawText` slice of the token text.

Review-impact corrections worth remembering:

- **Arena orphans cannot change checker output today.** The sole
  production full-arena scan (`checker/src/access.rs`
  `js_assignment_candidates_for_property`) is JS-file-gated and consumed
  through an idempotent boolean `any`; speculation orphans are duplicates
  of committed nodes. Memory waste + future risk, not a live bug.
- **`import defer` was dropped, not folded** — the parser discarded the
  phase; `is_type_only` was already false for defer (a stale comment in
  `modules.rs` claimed otherwise). `phase_modifier` is now stored;
  nothing consumed it before, so capturing it is purely additive.
